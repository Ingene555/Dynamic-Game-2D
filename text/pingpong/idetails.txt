PingPong game made by LTS

The objective is to prevent the ball from coming out of your side.

The point index is 1.

Mode:	>>VS AI
	>>VS Friend
As well you can be Left vs AI or Right vs AI

The difficulty levelmis optional; the harder the level, the faster the ball goes and the smaller the bar
Difficulty:	>>Easy
		>>Normal
		>>Hard
		>>Very hard

The player who begins can be chosen :Player1(Left) or Player2(Right)
Player1(Left Player) controlled by "W" & "S", Player2(Right Player) controlled by "UP" & "DOWN"

Have fun!
